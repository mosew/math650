PREDICTING 2000 PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION RESULTS AT THE COUNTY LEVEL USING PUBLIC DATA

HYPOTHESIS: County-level aggregate census data should be decent predictors of a county's political leaning.

PREDICTORS: Selected 2000 US Census data aggregated at the county level.
TARGET: Percentage of each county voting for George W. Bush in 2000 (or this percentage thresholded at 0.5, to make a classification problem.)

MODELS: Several. See fittingmodels.R.
